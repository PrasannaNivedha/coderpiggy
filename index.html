I'm Coder Piggy


A curious little porker with a big dream and even bigger glasses. While most pigs are happy rolling in the mud, I roll up my sleeves and dive into lines of code.


I wasn’t born in Silicon Valley — I was raised in a small pen on a farm, surrounded by haystacks and hacking thoughts. I discovered my passion for coding the day I accidentally stepped on a keyboard and saw colors move on the screen. Since then, there’s been no turning back.


I may not have thumbs, but I’ve mastered HTML, CSS, and I’m sniffing around JavaScript. I build websites, solve problems, and believe that great ideas can come from the unlikeliest of places — even a pigsty.


So if you’re looking for code with creativity, heart, and a touch of farm-fresh genius, you’re in the right place.
